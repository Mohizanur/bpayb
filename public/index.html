Frequently Asked Questions

How does Birr Pay work?

Birr Pay acts as a subscription management platform where you can purchase and manage various digital services using Ethiopian Birr. We handle the international payments and provide you with seamless access to global services.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money, and major Ethiopian payment platforms to make it convenient for all users.

How long does it take to activate my subscription?

Most subscriptions are activated within 24 hours of payment confirmation. Some services may be activated immediately upon successful payment.

Can I manage multiple subscriptions?

Yes, you can manage multiple subscriptions from a single Birr Pay account. Our dashboard provides a comprehensive view of all your active services.

What happens when my subscription is about to expire?

We'll send you notifications before your subscription expires, giving you time to renew and continue enjoying uninterrupted service.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect all payment information and personal data.

What if I need help with my subscription?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to help with any subscription-related questions or issues you may have.

Can I cancel or modify my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your subscription through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.
